Der Stephansdom

The St. Stephen's Cathedral is the heart of vienna. It is in the center of the first dirstrict and very popular among tourists. It is very impressive and a Must See in Vienna but because of that it is very crowded there. You can entry the cathedral without paying and directly below it lie the spooky catacombs of Vienna. However, to go down there you have to book a tour.
